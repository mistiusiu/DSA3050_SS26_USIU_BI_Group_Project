    DAX Measures

    Overview

    We developed a set of DAX measures to support business questions. Measures are organized by functional category and stored in a dedicated _Measures table for clean model organization.

    Complete Measures List

    # Measure Name Category Description
    1 Total Posts Aggregation Count of all published posts
    2 Total Likes Aggregation Sum of all likes received
    3 Total Retweets Aggregation Sum of all retweets/shares
    4 Total Engagement Aggregation Sum of all engagement actions (likes + retweets + replies + quotes)
    5 Avg Engagement per Post Aggregation Average engagement per post
    6 Avg Engagement Aggregation General average engagement
    7 Avg Positive Sentiment Aggregation Average positive sentiment score
    8 Avg Negative Sentiment Aggregation Average negative sentiment score
    9 Engagement MTD Time Intelligence Month-to-date total engagement
    10 Engagement YoY % Change Percentage Change Year-over-year growth rate
    11 Engagement QoQ % Change Percentage Change Quarter-over-quarter growth rate
    12 Engagement Rate KPI Engagement relative to audience size
    13 Engagement Growth Trend KPI Change in growth rate (acceleration/deceleration)
    14 Net Sentiment Score KPI Positive minus negative sentiment balance
    15 State Engagement Rank Ranking State ranking by total engagement (1 = highest)
    16 Post Type Share of Engagement Advanced Percentage of total engagement by post type
    17 Top Post Type Advanced Highest-performing content format
    18 High Positive Sentiment Engagement Advanced Avg engagement for posts with above-average positive sentiment
    19 Low Positive Sentiment Engagement Advanced Avg engagement for posts with below-average positive sentiment
